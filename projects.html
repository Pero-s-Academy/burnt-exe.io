Projects @ Burnt-Exe.io

Our Projects

At Burnt-Exe.io, we are dedicated to providing hands-on learning experiences through a variety of innovative projects. Here are some of the key projects we offer:

Phishing Simulator Learning Platform

A comprehensive platform designed to educate and train individuals and organizations on recognizing and preventing phishing attacks through interactive simulations.

LMS (Learning Management System)

A robust learning management system for efficient course delivery and tracking. This project includes features such as course enrollment, progress tracking, and certification.

Pero's Academy

An initiative to provide quality education and training in various tech domains. This project involves creating and managing courses, developing learning paths, and engaging with the community.

Business Model Innovation for 4Levels

A strategic analysis project focusing on developing innovative business models for 4Levels to enhance their market presence and operational efficiency.

Email Template Design Challenge for 4Levels Solutions

This project involves designing and developing email templates for marketing and communication purposes for 4Levels Solutions.

Cyber Retaliator Solutions Strategy Development Project

Developing strategic plans and solutions for Cyber Retaliator to improve their cybersecurity offerings and market strategy.
